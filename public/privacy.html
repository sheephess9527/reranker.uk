Privacy

reranker.uk is a static educational site. Here is what happens to your data when you browse or use the live demo.

Static pages

Guide and model pages are plain HTML served from Cloudflare. We do not run first-party analytics, tracking pixels, or sign-in flows on this site.

Live demo

The reranking demo runs entirely in your browser with transformers.js. Your query and candidate passages are processed locally on your device. They are not sent to reranker.uk servers or any rerank API.

On first use, the demo downloads open-weight model files from the Hugging Face CDN (via jsDelivr). Those weights are cached in your browser storage for faster repeat visits. Share links encode your query and passages in the URL — only share them with people you trust, since anyone with the link can read that text.

Third parties

  • Cloudflare — hosts static files and may log standard request metadata (IP, user agent) per their privacy policy.
  • jsDelivr / Hugging Face — serves transformers.js and ONNX model weights when you open the demo.
  • GitHub — if you follow the “View source” link, GitHub’s terms apply.
